cryptography and JavaScript

Motivation

JavaScript is (one of?) the most used programming-languages all over the world and webbrowser are THE "runtime-environment" of the web. There seems to be no evidence, that this will change in the near future, so we have to ensure, that its possible, to use them in a secure way.
